My Plans

(After Graduating)

"I buried all the pain. Used to feel so ashamed. Until I learned that you can't change the past, just choose the frame. So I'll keep writing songs, until they know my name. 'Cause there's a kid out there that's hearing this that's in that place." Jake Segura (Citizen Soldier) - Tattoos

My plans after graduating from Neumont University in September 2027 are to pursue a career in software development or game development, hopefully utilizing the Enterprise Projects to get a job with one of the companies that I work with.

Many of my peers have expressed interest in pursuing indie game development, after watching the constant flux of the AAA game industry and its treatment of both employees and consumers. After consideration, I have seen their points and might do the same after careful financial planning. We have, also, thought of starting an indie game studio with the graduates of my Cohort if possible.

A career that would greatly interest me would be a remote-work job that allowed me to stay at home most days and work on my own projects during my free time. This would allow me to focus on building my own games and software in my off time, while still earning a steady income to support myself.
